Stawberry Mojito Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Mint Leaves
  • White Rum
  • Dark Rum
  • Syrum
  • Lime

Instructions

  1. Prepare a high ball glass
  2. Muddle mint leaves
  3. One teaspoon of brown sugar
  4. Fill 0.5 oz stawberry syrup
  5. Fill 0.5 oz lime
  6. Fill 1 oz of dark rum
  7. Fill 1 oz of whit rumspirit
  8. Fill ice
  9. Pour Soda on Top
  10. Garnic with Lime Wheel and Stawberry
