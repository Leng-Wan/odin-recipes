Pink Lady Cocktail Recipes

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 oz of milk
  • 1 oz of simple syrup
  • 1 oz of lime
  • 1.5 oz of Gin

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Boston shaker
  2. Fill simple syrup
  3. Fill lime
  4. Fill milk
  5. Fill spirit
  6. Fill ice
  7. Shake for 3 sec
  8. Pour into the martini glass
  9. Garnic with Lime Wheel
